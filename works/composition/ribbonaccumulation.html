Ribbon Accumulation

リボン集積 (2022)

for violin

Duration: 4min.

[SCORE]

composed for the project mumyo 'Gothic and Lolita'

premiered by Tatsuki Narita

created in Tokyo, Nov.2.2022

This music is based on the foundation of European traditional functional harmony, weaving together themes of hierarchy, order, discipline, and deviation. It takes the girlish icon of a ribbon and abstracts it into the intangible realm of sound through sensory perception, continuously painting an auditory canvas. The ribbon is layered with Western musical tradition, delving into the depths of its sublimity to reflect upon various elements such as violence, cruelty, and grotesqueness that reside within.

ヨーロッパ伝統の機能和声を下地に、主従と秩序、規律、その逸脱を紡いだ音楽。リボンという少女的アイコンを、音という目に見えないものに抽象化し延々と描き続けることで作られている。リボンを西洋の音の伝統に重ね、崇高さの内側にある暴力やグロテスクを共に反芻していく。
